🌬️ 3 Simple Breathing Exercises

  • Box Breathing: Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 4, exhale for 4, hold for 4.
  • 4-7-8 Breathing: Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 7, exhale for 8.
  • Alternate Nostril Breathing: Breathe in through one nostril, switch, and exhale.

Try these simple mindfulness activities each day:

Day 4 Listen to a guided meditation
Day 5 Write down your emotions in a journal
Day 6 Limit social media & be fully present
Day 7 Express gratitude to someone special
